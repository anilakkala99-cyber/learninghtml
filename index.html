4.INTRA VERSION-EXTRA VERSION These terms are normally associated with an individual sociability& inter personal orientation. Extroverts are gregarious, sociable where introverts are shy, quiet, retiring (unwilling to be noticed). Extroverts are more suitable for positions that require considerable interaction with others. Whereas introverts are more inclined to excel at tasks that require thought, analytical skills. Not surprisingly managerial positions are dominated by extroverts.
